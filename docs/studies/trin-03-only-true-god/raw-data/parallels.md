# Cross-Testament Parallels

- John 17:3 → 1 John 5:20 (nt, vocabulary + theological — same author uses 'true God' (alethinos theos) language with Jesus as nearest antecedent)
- John 17:3 → 1 Thessalonians 1:9 (nt, vocabulary — 'true God' (alethinos theos) explicitly contrasting with idols)
- 1 Corinthians 8:6 → Ephesians 4:4-6 (nt, structural — same heis pattern distributed across Spirit, Lord, God)
- 1 Corinthians 8:6 → Malachi 2:10 (ot, vocabulary — 'one father, one God created' (Perriman's alternative background))
- 1 Corinthians 8:6 → Deuteronomy 6:4 (ot, structural + vocabulary — Shema's heis theos and heis kyrios distributed in Paul's confession)
- John 1:1 → Hebrews 1:10 (nt, theological — both attribute creation to the Son)
- John 17:5 → John 17:24 (nt, vocabulary + theological — pre-existent glory and pre-creation love)
- Isaiah 45:23 → Philippians 2:10-11 (nt, direct quotation — YHWH's exclusive oath applied to Jesus)
- Isaiah 44:6 → Revelation 1:17 (nt, vocabulary — 'first and last' YHWH title applied to Jesus)
- Isaiah 44:6 → Revelation 22:13 (nt, vocabulary — 'first and last' + 'Alpha and Omega' applied to Jesus)
- Joel 2:32 → Romans 10:13 (nt, direct quotation — 'call on the name of YHWH' applied to Jesus)
- Psalm 102:25-27 → Hebrews 1:10-12 (nt, direct quotation — YHWH-creator psalm applied to the Son)
- Psalm 45:6-7 → Hebrews 1:8-9 (nt, direct quotation — 'Thy throne, O God' applied to the Son)
- Isaiah 43:11 → Titus 2:13 (nt, theological — 'beside me no saviour' + Jesus called soter under Granville Sharp)
- 1 Corinthians 8:6 → Romans 11:36 (nt, vocabulary — ek + dia used of the Father alone, collapsing ontological hierarchy argument)
- 1 Corinthians 8:6 → Colossians 1:16 (nt, vocabulary + theological — en/dia/eis all applied to Christ for creation)
- John 20:28 → John 17:3 (nt, intra-Gospel — same author records 'only true God' (17:3) and 'my Lord and my God' (20:28))
- Isaiah 6:1-5 → John 12:41 (nt, theological — John identifies Isaiah's YHWH vision with Jesus's glory)
